About Us!

Who We Are

Welcome to the Feline Advocacy Initiative (FAI), where our passion for feline welfare fuels our commitment to creating a world where every cat thrives. Established in 2023, FAI is more than an organization; it's a community of advocates, caregivers, and cat enthusiasts dedicated to nurturing lives, one purr at a time.

Vision And Mission

To envision a world where every cat, irrespective of circumstance, enjoys a life of health, happiness, and humane treatment

Dedicated to championing feline welfare globally, we strive to be the leading advocates, educators, and caregivers, ensuring every cat's well-being through compassion, advocacy, and community engagement.

How To Help?

Spread Awareness: Share our advocacy on social media, engage in conversations about responsible cat ownership, and encourage others to join the cause.

Volunteer Locally: Contribute your time and skills by volunteering at local shelters, participating in community cat care initiatives, or assisting during adoption events.

Donate Supplies: Support our efforts by donating essential cat supplies, such as food, bedding, and medical items, to local shelters and community cat care programs.
