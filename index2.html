What We Do!

Initiatives

Objective: Enhance cat well-being through accessible healthcare and responsible pet ownership.

Objective: Implement humane strategies for community cat management.

Involvements

National Cat Welfare Advocacy: Objective: Champion cat welfare legislation on a national scale. Involvement: Legislation Research: Analyze and propose amendments for comprehensive cat welfare laws. Legislator Collaboration: Partner with lawmakers for impactful legislation and public support. Awareness Campaign: Engage nationwide for citizen advocacy and policy change.

Local Shelter Support: Objective: Assist and enhance local shelter efforts for feline welfare. Involvement: Donation Drives: Organize drives for cat supplies to benefit local shelters. Volunteer Programs: Mobilize volunteers for daily shelter operations and cat care. Staff Training Workshops: Educate shelter staff on optimal feline care practices.

School Education Program: Objective: Integrate feline welfare education in schools. Involvement: Curriculum Development: Create age-appropriate modules for responsible pet ownership. Interactive Workshops: Conduct engaging sessions with therapy cats to instill empathy. Community Outreach: Extend the program to involve parents and the wider community.
